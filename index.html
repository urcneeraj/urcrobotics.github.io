Key Features — URC Commercial Cleaning Robot

Autonomous Navigation

LiDAR + visual odometry + SLAM for accurate mapping, dynamic re-routing, and repeatable coverage plans.

Adaptive Cleaning Modes

Edge-scrub, spot-clean, slow-dry, and wet-dry with configurable water dispense and brush pressure.

Safety & Obstacle Handling

Multi-layered safety: bumpers, 3D ToF sensors, audible alerts, and remote E-stop via fleet manager.

Telematics & Dashboard

Cloud dashboard for schedules, health monitoring, route playback and OTA firmware delivery.

Water & Energy Efficient

Optimized dispensing, regenerative braking for energy recovery and low-consumption motors.

Modular Service Design

Quick-swap scrub heads, easily accessible filters and swappable battery modules.
