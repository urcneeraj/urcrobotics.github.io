About URC Robotics

Building India's Future with Intelligent Automation

Our Vision

At URC Robotics, we believe robotics is not just about machines — it’s about empowering people and industries. Our vision is to make India a global hub for affordable and reliable robotic solutions that improve everyday life.

Our Story

Founded in Rajkot, Gujarat, URC Robotics began as a student project and grew into a company with a mission to bring automation to real-world Indian environments. From commercial cleaning robots to intelligent service systems, our focus has always been on building technology that is both advanced and practical.

Our Expertise

Our multidisciplinary team brings together expertise in AI, embedded systems, mechanical design, and software engineering. Each product is built and tested in-house to ensure durability and adaptability to Indian conditions.
